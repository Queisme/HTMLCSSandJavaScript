    Six Different Automatic numberings

  1. Decimal - decimal or decimal-leading-zero
  2. Roman - lower-roman or upper-roman
  3. Latin - lower-latin or upper-lating or lower-alpha or upper-alpha
  4. Greek - lower-greek
  5. Georgian - georgian
  6. Armenian - armenian
    7. Bonus:
  7. None - to suppress numbering
